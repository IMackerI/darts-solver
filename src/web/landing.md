# How do I use it?

Excelent question.
You being here is half of the work done.

## Step 1: You calibrate
Throw at center.
Click where you hit.
Click Estimate.
*Done*

## Step 2: You find out
Click at a tab.
Find out where should you throw.
It is that easy.

the individual modes and settings are in the corresponding tabs :)


--- now the individual tabs (the text under the name) ---
Maximize Points:
Find out how many points can you expect when aiming somewhere.

Minimize Throws:
Find out where to aim for this particular _score_.
This tells you: "If you throw here and then follow our _optimal_ advice for the rest of the game, this many throws can you expect to finish."

Minimize Rounds:
Find out where to aim for this particular _game state_.
This is the strongest solver of them all. This is the _advanced_ mode. It takes into account the whole round state.
This means it is the slowest of them all. It won't recalculate unless you have this state solved already or you click "Solve".
